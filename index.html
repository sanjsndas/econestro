About Eco-nestro

We are a team of passionate gardeners dedicated to creating sustainable, beautiful green spaces in urban environments.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Eco-nestro began as a small community gardening initiative in Lower Manhattan. What started as a passion project among neighbors has grown into New York City's premier organic gardening service provider.

Our mission is to reconnect urban dwellers with nature by creating sustainable, low-maintenance green spaces that thrive in the city environment. We believe everyone deserves access to beautiful, healthy plants regardless of their living situation.

All our services use 100% organic methods and materials. We never use synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, ensuring your garden is safe for children, pets, and the environment.
