Contact FAQs

What's the best way to get a quick response?

For the fastest response, call us during business hours at (212) 555-0199. If your inquiry isn't urgent, our contact form typically receives a response within 24 business hours. During peak seasons (spring and early summer), response times may be slightly longer.

Do you offer emergency gardening services?

Yes, we offer emergency services for situations like storm-damaged trees, hazardous plant conditions, or irrigation system failures. Call our main number and press 1 for emergency services. Additional fees may apply for after-hours emergency calls.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

Our office is open to visitors Monday-Friday 9am-5pm, but we strongly recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure the appropriate team member is available to assist you. Our design consultants often work on-site and may not be available for walk-ins.

How do I get a quote for services?

You can request a quote through our contact form by selecting "Request a Quote" as your subject. For more accurate estimates, we recommend scheduling an on-site consultation. Our online quote calculator can provide rough estimates for basic services.

What information should I include in my message?

Please include your contact information, property address (if service-related), details about your inquiry, and any relevant photos if discussing specific plants or garden areas. The more details you provide, the better we can assist you.
